Abstract: Survey distinguishing Long CoT from Short CoT with a taxonomy of reasoning paradigms. Key characteristics of Long CoT: deep reasoning, extensive exploration, feasible reflection. Discusses "overthinking" and "inference-time scaling" debates. Covers RL entropy mechanism in Long CoT training.

Key takeaway: comprehensive survey covering the o1/DeepSeek-R1-style long reasoning paradigm and the open debates around overthinking/underthinking.
