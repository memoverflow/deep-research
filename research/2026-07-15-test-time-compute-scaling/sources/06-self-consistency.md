Abstract: Proposes self-consistency decoding: sample a diverse set of reasoning paths instead of greedy decoding, then select the most consistent answer by majority vote over sampled paths. Boosts CoT performance significantly on GSM8K (+17.9%), SVAMP (+11.0%), AQuA (+12.2%), StrategyQA (+6.4%), ARC-challenge (+3.9%).

Key takeaway: earliest widely-cited "repeated sampling + voting" test-time scaling technique, predecessor to Best-of-N with verifiers.
