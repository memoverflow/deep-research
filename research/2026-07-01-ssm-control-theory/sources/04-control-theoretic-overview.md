Abstract summary: In recent years, there has been a growing interest in integrating linear state-space models (SSM) in deep neural network architectures of foundation models. This is exemplified by the recent success of Mamba, showing better performance than the state-of-the-art Transformer architectures in language tasks. Paper provides a control-theoretic perspective connecting classical SSM theory (controllability, observability, LTI systems) to modern deep learning SSM architectures like S4/Mamba.

Key facts extracted:
- Frames SSM foundation models explicitly through classical control theory lens
- Discusses linear time-invariant (LTI) system theory as basis
- Connects discretization methods to stability guarantees
