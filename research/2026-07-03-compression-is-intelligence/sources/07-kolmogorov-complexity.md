Kolmogorov complexity of an object is length of shortest program that outputs it. Based on theorem discovered by Ray Solomonoff (1960), part of his invention of algorithmic probability / Solomonoff induction. Kolmogorov complexity is not computable — no algorithm can determine the shortest program for arbitrary data (related to halting problem).
